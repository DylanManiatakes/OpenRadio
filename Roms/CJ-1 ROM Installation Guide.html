CJ-1 ROM Installation Guide

1. Download and Install WinRAR

Visit WinRAR Website and install WinRAR on your computer. Note: Do not use Windows Zip extractor.

2. Choose and Download the ROM

Regular Rom with Google Services Non-Google Rom

3. Extract the ROM

Use WinRAR to extract Part 1. The extraction will automatically combine both parts into a single folder.

4. Download and Install Device Drivers

Download Device Drivers and install them to ensure proper communication with your CJ-1 device.

5. Download FlashTool

Download FlashTool

6. Flash the ROM to Your CJ-1

  1. Launch FlashTool.
  2. Select the scatter file located in the extracted ROM folder.
  3. Ensure your CJ-1 is powered off.
  4. In FlashTool, set the dropdown menu (below the Authentication File Box) to “Firmware Upgrade”.
  5. Click the green Download button. FlashTool will appear unresponsive (this is normal).
  6. Connect your powered-off CJ-1 to your computer using a USB cable.
    • FlashTool should begin the ROM upgrade process within a few seconds.
    • The process takes approximately 5 minutes to complete.

7. Power On the CJ-1

After flashing is complete, disconnect the device and power it on. The first boot after the upgrade may take 3–4 minutes.
