How to Restore IMEI Number on ComJoT Radio

IMPORTANT: Ensure settings match exactly as indicated. DO NOT CHANGE THE DATABASE INFO AT THE BOTTOM.
IMPORTANT: Make sure your ComJoT Radio is turned completely off and NOT plugged into the computer.

Step 1 - Download and Install Device Drivers

Before beginning, download and install the necessary device drivers from the link below:

Download Device Drivers

Once downloaded, run the installer and follow the on-screen prompts to complete installation.

Step 2 - Download the IMEI Restoration Tool

Download IMEI Restoration Tool

Unzip the downloaded file, then unRAR the SN Writer App.

Step 3 - Configure SN Writer App

Open the SN Writer app (NOT the file ending in "_dd"). Click "Smart Config" and ensure your settings match exactly with the provided screenshot. Click "Save" to return to the main window.

Step 4 - Turn Off Your Device

Ensure your ComJoT Radio is turned OFF completely.

Step 5 - Enter IMEI Numbers

Click "Start" and enter your two IMEI numbers (from the back of your radio or the box).

Step 6 - Connect Your Device

While device is turned OFF, Connect your ComJoT Radio to your computer via USB cable. You should hear a Windows notification sound. In seconds, your radio will show a black screen with white text at the bottom.

Step 7 - Confirmation

A confirmation message indicating successful IMEI restoration will appear in the SN Writer.

If you encounter issues, carefully repeat each step.
